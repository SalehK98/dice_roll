Game Instructions

  • In your turn - roll the dice(at least once) and accumulate the result in "current".
  • You can roll agin or click "Hold" to save the points from "Current" and end the turn.
  • Note if you get 6-6 you will lose all points from "current" and the turn will go to your opponent.
  • if you manged to reach exactly the target score - you win, if you passed it you lose...
  • player one starts first - on the left side.
